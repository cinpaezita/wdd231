Project Site Plan

Overview

Site Name: Cookieverse

The name "Cookieverse" represents a delightful world of cookies where customers can explore a universe of unique flavors and treats. It conveys creativity, warmth, and a sense of adventure, making it appealing to cookie lovers of all ages. The name also reflects the entrepreneurial spirit behind the project, aiming to establish a memorable and welcoming brand identity.

Optional domain availability: cookiverse.com

Site Purpose

The Cookieverse website serves as a hub for cookie enthusiasts by showcasing a variety of handcrafted cookies, offering details on ingredients, flavors, and prices. Visitors can explore the top-selling cookies on the home page, browse the full catalog of delicious treats, and place orders conveniently through an interactive form. The site aims to provide a seamless experience for customers, blending mouthwatering visuals, detailed product information, and easy access to ordering.

Scenarios

  • What types of cookies are available, and how can I order them?

    • Visitors want to explore the variety of cookies offered on the site, including flavors, ingredients, and prices, and need guidance on how to place an order seamlessly.

  • What are the top-selling cookies, and what makes them special?

    • Customers are interested in knowing which cookies are the most popular and why, including unique flavors, premium ingredients, or special preparation techniques.
